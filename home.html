Mango ice cream recipe

Ingredients: - 2 large ripe mangoes (or 1 ½ cups mango puree) - 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk - 2 cups heavy cream (chilled) - 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional) - A pinch of salt (optional, to enhance the flavor)

Instructions:
1. Prepare the mango puree: - Peel and chop the ripe mangoes. Blend the mango chunks into a smooth puree using a blender or food processor. Set aside.
2. Whip the cream: - In a large bowl, whip the chilled heavy cream using a hand mixer or whisk until soft peaks form. This should take about 3-5 minutes. Make sure not to over-whip.
3. Combine the ingredients: - Gently fold in the sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract (if using), and mango puree into the whipped cream. Stir until well combined, but don’t over-mix to keep the light and airy texture.
4. Freeze: - Pour the mixture into an airtight container. Cover it with plastic wrap or a lid. - Freeze for at least 6 hours or overnight until firm.
5. Serve: - Once the ice cream is fully set, scoop and serve your creamy mango ice cream!

Best Burger Recipe

Just like a regular beef burger, you don’t want to over-complicate a turkey burger. This recipe will show you how to make the best turkey burgers, one that you may even prefer to the usual beef variety!

Ingredients in Turkey Burgers
Lean ground – This is where it all begins in making those turkey burgers.
Medium onion – Grating it distributes that onion juice and keeps big chunks from messing up the texture.
Worcestershire sauce – This will add more of a beefy flavor to your turkey patty recipe so no one will realize you’re using turkey instead of beef. It also helps ensure you end up with juicy turkey burgers.
Mayonnaise – Unites your turkey together while adding moisture.
Ketchup – Like the mayo, but with a richer flavor to mimic the taste of beef.
Salt and pepper – The only turkey burger seasoning you’ll need. Add to your preference.
